Grep & Regex

grep

Global Regular Expression Print. A command-line utility for searching plain-text data sets for lines that match a regular expression.

Recursive (-r)

Searching inside specific files and also inside all files in its sub-folders.

Ignore Case (-i)

Perform a search that is not case-sensitive.

Regular Expression (Regex)

A sequence of characters that specifies a search pattern.
