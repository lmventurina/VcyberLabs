CIA Triad & Authentication

CYBER.ORG Range Worksheet

Objective

Understand the core principles of information security: Confidentiality, Integrity, and Availability (The CIA Triad), and explore methods of Authentication.

Part 1: The CIA Triad

For each scenario, identify which part of the CIA Triad is being violated or protected.

Scenario 1

A hacker changes the grades in the student database from a 'C' to an 'A'.

Scenario 2

A Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) attack takes down the school website during finals week.

Scenario 3

A student reads a teacher's private email containing exam questions.

Part 2: Authentication Factors

Classify the following authentication methods into the three categories: Something you Know, Something you Have, or Something you Are.

Part 3: Reflection
